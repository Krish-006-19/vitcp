Welcome to the Government of India - Financial Overview

Your one-stop portal for all financial services, policies, and updates from the Government of India.

Overview of Financial Information

The Government of India provides a range of financial services to individuals, businesses, and state-run organizations. This portal offers easy access to financial policies, budget announcements, and taxation details, along with other financial resources to help citizens and investors.

The key areas covered in the financial domain are the national budget, economic policies, investment opportunities, taxation schemes, and public sector financial information.

Key Functions and Contributions


1.Economic Growth and Stability The Ministry formulates policies that drive India’s economic development, ensuring sustainable growth and resilience in global financial markets.
2.Efficient Taxation System Through the introduction of GST (Goods and Services Tax) and direct tax reforms, the Ministry has simplified taxation, increasing transparency and ease of doing business.
3.Financial Inclusion Initiatives like Jan Dhan Yojana, Digital Payments, and UPI have expanded financial access to millions, promoting digital transactions and economic empowerment.
4.Infrastructure and Investment BoostPolicies like Make in India, Atmanirbhar Bharat, and National Infrastructure Pipeline have strengthened India's investment climate and global competitiveness.
5.Budgetary and Fiscal Management The Union Budget, crafted by the Ministry, focuses on social welfare, economic reforms, and financial prudence to balance growth and fiscal discipline.
6.Global Financial Leadership India, under the Finance Ministry’s leadership, actively participates in G20, IMF, and World Bank, shaping international financial policies.
Overall, the Finance Ministry of India plays a dynamic role in fostering economic resilience, digital transformation, and inclusive growth, positioning the country as a major global financial powerhouse.
