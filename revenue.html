Department of Revenue





Key Responsibilities of the Department:

  • Tax Policy & Administration:
    Formulating and implementing direct (Income Tax, Corporate Tax) and indirect (GST, Customs, Excise) tax policies.
  • Customs & Excise Duties:
    Regulating import/export duties and ensuring customs enforcement.
  • Goods & Services Tax (GST) Implementation:
    Coordinating with the GST Council to streamline and implement GST policies across states.
  • Financial Intelligence & Anti-Money Laundering:
    Monitoring suspicious financial transactions through the Financial Intelligence Unit (FIU-IND).
