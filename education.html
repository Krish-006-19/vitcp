Introduction

The Education Budget of India is a critical component of national development. It determines the allocation of funds to primary, secondary, and higher education institutions. The budget plays a vital role in improving literacy rates, infrastructure, teacher training, research, and digital learning initiatives. Education is a fundamental pillar for economic growth and social progress, and adequate funding ensures equitable access to quality education for all.
