Introduction

The Healthcare Budget of India is a crucial component of national development. It determines the allocation of funds for public health services, infrastructure, medical research, insurance schemes, and pandemic preparedness. Healthcare funding ensures better access to medical facilities, advanced treatments, and an overall improved quality of life for citizens.
